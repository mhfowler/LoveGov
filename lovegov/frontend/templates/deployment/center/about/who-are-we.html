The Team

 

our mission

LoveGov’s mission is to develop tools that facilitate democracy. We are committed to non-partisanship, government transparency and the right to individual privacy.

We believe that active and informed citizens make a better democracy. Political information should be easy to access, understand, and act upon. Current politics removes people from the discourse because it’s too frustrating, complicated and time consuming. By simplifying the process of staying knowledgeable and being involved, we hope everyone can participate in politics and actually make a difference.


Non-Partisanship

LoveGov has no political agenda; we try to ensure that none of our tools, mechanisms, or questions favor one worldview over another. However, we realize that team members have their own political views, and we’re consciously trying to keep those views out of the design of the platform. We will limit expressing our political opinions to our own user accounts. If you feel that we’re being partisan in any way, please share your concerns with us.

Government Transparency

Voters need to know if their representatives’ actions are consistent with their claims, what impact legislation has on their lives, and how the political process actually works—otherwise they cannot be effective voters and hold their representatives accountable for their actions.

Right to Individual Privacy

Democracies are founded on the principle that citizens privately cast their ballots. People need to have the option of expressing their views without fear of consequences from their community or government, otherwise minority viewpoints will not be expressed. This is why we have a strong privacy policy. We will NEVER sell or share your personal information. On LoveGov, citizens are able to express themselves while controlling their information.

Our privacy settings are simple. In public mode, everyone can see your activity. In private mode, nobody can see your activity—you are anonymous. That’s it. We don’t have custom privacy settings because they are too convoluted, and ultimately don’t completely protect your private information. If you are doing something on LoveGov that you don’t want anyone to know about, then do it in private mode.

Please see our blog for further discussions of our mission and policies and to learn about what features will come next!
