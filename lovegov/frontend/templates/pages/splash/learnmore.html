Participating is easy.

Get Informed

Democracy works best when citizens know what's happening in their government. With all the information on the Internet and in traditional media outlets, you’d think keeping current with political issues would be easier than ever. But, it’s not. We want to change that. At LoveGov, we're simplifying politics by filtering information relevant to your interests. You’ll be able to stay on top of the news, track legislation, and mark upcoming elections, all in one place .

Build Connections

Getting informed is the first step towards making a difference, but a single voice — no matter how informed — is hard to hear in a loud crowd of people. LoveGov filters the noise. Through our social networking tools, you'll be able to connect with like-minded folks as well as with advocacy groups, politicians, and government agencies involved in the issues you care about. Together, you’ll find ways to address and solve your country's problems.

Enact Change

When informed people unite, change happens. But, we don’t think it’s enough to simply bring people together. At LoveGov, we provide you and your new allies with powerful tools to more easily enact change. You’ll find resources to assist you in organizing events, writing and circulating petitions, engaging your representatives and much, much more. Your views matter and your activity on LoveGov can and will contribute to creating a better democracy.
