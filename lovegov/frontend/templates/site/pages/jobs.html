Campus Liaison

Young politicos are the foundation of LoveGov. Our matching mechanism and group tools are perfect for student groups to recruit members, coordinate their efforts, take action, and promote their cause. We need you, the campus liaison, to get all the political student groups on LoveGov, and then through them, the rest of the student body.

We are looking for leaders that are passionate about the LoveGov cause. A large part of your job will simply be to speak directly to student group leaders, explaining how to use LoveGov, and getting them on it. You will need to be creative in seeding campuswide initiatives and doing guerrilla marketing. While this is an unpaid internship, we do accept proposals for funded marketing campaigns. Contact Alex for details.
