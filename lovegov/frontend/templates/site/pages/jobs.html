Campus Liaison

Young politicos are the foundation of LoveGov. Our matching mechanism and group tools are perfect for student groups to recruit members, coordinate their efforts, take action, and promote their cause. We need you, the campus liaison, to get your school's political and activist groups to have a presence on LoveGov and utilize our tools to promote their cause. You will be responsible for leading campus-wide initiatives that engage these groups and the student body via our website. As we move past the election and into one of the most pivotal legislative periods, both nationally and locally, you will also be coordinating with leaders across campuses to begin national initiatives.

This is an un-paid internship. We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers from high schools and universities across the country to join us in excting endeavor as we build this political plaform. You will target the issues that are important to your community and help build the political platform of the future. When young people are able to mobilize, coordinate their efforts, and be heard, change can actually happen. LoveGov provides the platform for that mobilization and spread of information. You will be the one to bring that platform to your school and enable our generation to claim its stake in history. Are you up for the challenge? If you are interested in being a liaison, please contact alex@lovegov.com.

Participating Campuses:

  • Brown University
  • Rhode Island School of Design
  • Suffolk University
  • Northeastern University
  • Fordham University
  • Milton Academy
  • Northfield Mount Hermon School
