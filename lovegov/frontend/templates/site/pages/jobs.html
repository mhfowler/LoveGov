Campus Representative

Young politicos are the foundation of LoveGov. At the core of service is the ability for groups to recruit members, coordinate their efforts, take action, and promote their cause. We are focusing on college student groups as the primary avenue into these young, politically active communities. We need you, the campus representative, to get all the political student groups on LoveGov, and then through them, the entire student body.


We are looking for leaders. Already knowing the leaders on campus is important, but what really matters is that you are excited about and devoted to the LoveGov cause. Creativity is also key. There are many ways groups can use our website, and there are just as many ways that you can get the word out there, or even start a campuswide initiative. Contact Alex for more information.
