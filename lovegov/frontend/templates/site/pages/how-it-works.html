What is LoveGov?

LoveGov is a political organizing platform and social network for both passionate and curious people to discover causes they care about, take action, and stay informed.

You answer questions, we match you with politicians, your city/state groups, organizations, and other LoveGov members based on your beliefs.

Here's how it works.
Find out where you stand
Define your views by taking stances on issues and weighting their importance. Instantly see how your congressman's voting record matches up to your positions.
Compare your beliefs with other people on LoveGov by hovering over their name or picture, and instantly see the percentage of answers you share. Or view their profile to see a detailed comparison.

Comparisons give you an at-a-glance comparison to your political world. The central % is your overall match. It is broken into the seven main topics that are each filled in equal to the match percentage.

Hover over any of the circles to see the match percentage and the total number of questions that went into the comparison.

Discover groups that share your passion
Join political parties, find groups in your area and get matched to organizations share your beliefs and interests. Or create your own group and gain visibility for your cause.
Advocate and express your beliefs
Answer questions and defend your positions, publicly support candidates, sign petitions, and debate the issues in discussion threads.
Share and collaborate with platform tools
Post news, discuss current events, poll your community, organize your group's efforts and coordinate with other groups.
Get facts, not just opinions
LoveGov aggregates publicly available data on legislation, voting records, elections, and other resources which can be easily browsed or referenced.
Stay relevant
By choosing what groups, regions, and elections to follow, you filter all the political noise down to what is directly relevant to you.
Keep your private views private
LoveGov users can post anything publicly or anonymously, a feature intended to encourage open, honest discussions. You can also answer questions privately, which will improve your matches to others, but not their matches to you. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.
See where the world stands
All responses to questions are publicly available, and anyone can post questions to LoveGov. Questions get organized geographically, so every region has its political discourse limited to the relevant community. By answering questions, users both have their views heard, and improve their matches on local, state and federal levels.
