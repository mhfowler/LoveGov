What is LoveGov?

LoveGov is an organizing platform and social network for both politically passionate and politically curious people to discover causes they care about, take action, and stay informed.

You answer questions, we match you with politicians, candidates, interest groups, and other LoveGov members based on your beliefs.

Here's how it works.
Find out where you stand
Answer questions about your beliefs and instantly get matched with candidates based on their voting record.
Stress what's important
Tell us what issues matter to you by weighing certain questions more than others, and we'll take that into account when computing your matches.
Match with friends and strangers
Compare your beliefs with other people on LoveGov by hovering over their name or picture, and instantly see the percentage of answers you share. Or view their profile to see a detailed comparison.

Comparisons help you understand visually at-a-glance how you compare to your political world. The % in the red circle in the middle is your overall similarity. This % is also how much the circle is filled in from the bottom.

The seven main topics function in the same way but you can hover over them to see the % value. Hover over any of the circles to see the total number of questions which created the comparison.

Discover groups that share your passion
Find groups with similar beliefs and interests based on the aggregate opinion of group members. Or create your own and gain visibility for your cause.
Advocate and express your beliefs
Publicly support candidates, sign petitions, and campaign for your interest group in discussion threads.
Share and collaborate with platform tools
Post news, discuss current events, and organize your group's efforts.
Get facts, not just opinions
LoveGov aggregates publicly available data on legislation, voting records, elections, and other resources which can be easily browsed or referenced. For instance, we show detailed information of every bill passed by or proposed to the US Congress during the past three congressional sessions.
Stay relevant
You can choose what groups and elections to follow, and we'll only show you content posted there. We'll also suggest local groups and content.
Keep your private views private
LoveGov users can post anything publicly or anonymously, a feature intended to encourage open, honest discussions. You can also answer questions privately, which will improve your matches to others, but not their matches to you. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.
Collect useful data
All question and response data is publicly available, and anyone can post questions or polls to LoveGov. Users have an incentive to answer questions because it improves their matches with other users.
