Why do I need an account? What are the benefits?

Signing up with your Facebook, Twitter, or email allows us to save your answers which we use to generate your world view.

By creating an account, you can:

  • Compare yourself with hundreds of politicians, legislators, and candidates running for office
  • Match yourself with friends and strangers
  • Get detailed break-downs of how you match with other users by topic
  • Use community organizing tools to share news, host discussions, create petitions, and more
