What is LoveGov?

LoveGov will inform you on the issues, keep you current with legislation that you care about, and connect you with your representatives. We're making it easy to get involved.

what is a comparison?

Comparisons help you understand visually at-a-glance how you compare to your political world. As you may see, the % in the red circle in the middle is your overall similarity. This % is also how much the circle is filled in from the bottom.

The seven main topics function in the same way but you can hover over them to see the % value. Hover over any of the circles to see the total number of questions which created the comparison.
