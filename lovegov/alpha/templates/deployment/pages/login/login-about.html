What is LoveGov?

LoveGov will inform you on the issues, keep you current with legislation that you care about, and connect you with your representatives. We're making it easy to get involved.

1

answer questions

Define your political beliefs by answering questions about political issues. Questions are based off legislation that has appeared in congress over the past few years.

2

find & compare

See where you stand politically in your social world. Compare yourself with presidential candidates, congress, as well as your friends.

3

create & discuss

Make petitions, share news, and debate the issues with others. As our site grows you will have more ways to express yourself and participate in your democracy.
